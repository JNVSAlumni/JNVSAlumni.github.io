Code of Conduct for Alumni Association Formal WhatsApp Group

Below set of rules are applicable for all the posts / contents posted into Alumni WhatsApp Group

  1. Of course everything about JNV
  2. Guidelines related to career
  3. Achievements of our alumni
  4. Important news/events related to alumni like marriage, job or other
  5. Posts related to recruitments, vacancies
  6. Donation to Alumni Funds
  7. Posts related to genuine helps to students or alumni
  8. Creativities of our students and alumni
  9. Wish message only on the specified date of calendar, not advance wishes
  10. Based on the intensity of irrelevent post, a member can be warned or removed
